OCDAP Home Page

The Online Collaborative DNA Analysis Program (OCDAP) is a free, open-access program intended to provide visualization of cut sites in restriction mapping.
DNA sequences are obtained from the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI).
The compiled list of restriction enzymes are from Dr. Tom Hall's (NCSU) BioEdit restriction enzyme list.

The project can be accessed at GitHub.

The top link in the directory below will lead you to the main restriction mapping function.
